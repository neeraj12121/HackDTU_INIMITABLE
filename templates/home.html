What can you get from this?

Here are the cool features you can get from this web app.

Diagnosis

You will be able to see what is the possible disease you have. Take note that this may not be very accurate. Trust at your own risk.

Medication

You will be given suggestions of what medications you can take. This can serve as your first aid but may not apply for long periods of time. You may want to ask the opinion of an expert first before using it.

Specialists

You will be given a list of doctors near your area who can help you. These are the people who specialize in treating the disease that you were diagnosed with.
